— Questions —

You probably want to ask.

Is this speed dating?

No. There's no rotating chairs, no name tags, no buzzer. It's a beer hall full of people who are open to meeting people, with wristbands so you don't have to ask awkward questions. You can mingle, sit with your friends, ignore everyone — it's a Friday night.

I'm in a couple. Can I come?

Yes — and you should. The whole concept is "friends of friends." Couples are the best matchmakers. Bring the single friend you've been meaning to set up for the last three years. Show up wearing red wristbands so single people know.

Why "Dating in person"?

Because the best couples we know all started with a friend introducing them at a Friday-night thing. This is a bigger version of that — your group chat scaled up to a beer hall.

What's the catch?

None. It's free, it's 21+, no cover. We cap RSVPs at 100 because the curation is the whole point — we want a room full of friends-of-friends, not a public bar event.

Do I have to RSVP?

Yes — please. We're capped at 100, and it helps Schoolyard plan staffing and Fresh plan chair time. Walk-ins are welcome only if we haven't hit cap.

Is it 21+?

Yes. Schoolyard is a beer hall. Bring ID.

What should I wear?

Whatever you'd wear to a beer hall on a Friday night. We're not running a gala. Jeans are a yes. Cute top is a yes. The shirt you stole from your roommate is a yes. If you can't be trusted with your hair, Fresh's chair opens at 7:30.

What if I don't know anyone?

Then this is the event for you. Friends of friends means every group started with someone who showed up alone. Grab a wristband, grab a drink, you'll be talking to someone in five minutes.

Where exactly is Schoolyard?

Schoolyard Beer Hall, Denver. Confirm the exact address with the venue's website. We'll add a Google Maps link in the RSVP confirmation email.
